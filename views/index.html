Welcome to the Blog Away API

Steal other's thoughts and comments from Blog Away through our API.

Calls you can make include:

  • /api/posts - pulls all available blog posts
  • /api/post/[post_id] - pulls a specific blog post based on post_id
  • /api/comments/[post_id] - pulls all comments tied to a specific blog post based on post_id
  • /api/authors - pulls all authors listed on the site
  • /api/author/[author_id] - pulls a specific author based on author_id
